Exploring Principal Component Analysis with the Women Writers' Project

This visualization shows the plotting of 16th and 17th century texts by their first and second principal component. Users can toggle the different genres, centures, or authors to filter the data. Hovering over a point will bring up more information in a tooltip. Click the reset button to reset all toggled checkboxes.
